Introduction

I respectfully submit this online portfolio to support my application for tenure and promotion to the rank of Associate Professor in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Winona State University. This is in accordance with the requirements of the IFO/MnSCU Master Agreement, Article 22: Professional Development and Evaluation, and Article 25: Tenure, Promotion, and Non-Renewal.

In compiling this portfolio, I often find myself in a state of gratitude. I owe an immense amount to my colleagues at Winona State University in general, and to the the statistics and data science faculty in particular. They simultaneously inspire and challenge me, and I owe them my thanks. It’s been a great ride!
